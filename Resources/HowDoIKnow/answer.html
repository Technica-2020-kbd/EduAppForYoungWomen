How do I know if I'm getting my period?

Common signs

There are a couple symptoms that show you're going to get your period soon such as:

  • A mucus-like substance that you might see on your underwear
  • Physical changes to your body like your breasts and body shape
  • Cramps
  • Bloating
  • Back pain
  • Trouble sleeping
