About

Purpose

whatsmyperiod.online is a web application that aims to help young girls and women learn more about their bodies - particularly menstruation! We hope that by sharing the information and resources they need, young girls and women will feel more confident about themselves and their bodies. This project was created as a hackathon project for Technica 2020.
